Privacy Policy

Updated: August 28, 2025

1. Introduction

At Bloggy Tech, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website (www.bloggytech.com), interact with our services, or engage with our content. By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you submit our contact form or subscribe to our newsletter, we may collect your name, email address, and phone number (if provided).
  • Usage Data: We collect non-identifying information about your interactions with our website, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent, using cookies and analytics tools.
  • Third-Party Data: If you interact with social media links (e.g., Twitter, Facebook), we may receive data from those platforms, subject to their privacy policies.

    • 3. How We Use Your Information

    Your information helps us improve our services and provide a personalized experience. We use it to:

    • Respond to inquiries submitted via our contact form.
    • Send newsletters, updates, or promotional content (with your consent).
    • Analyze website usage to enhance content and user experience.
    • Ensure the security and functionality of our website.

    We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

    4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    We use cookies to track user activity and improve website performance. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect site functionality. We also use Google Analytics to collect anonymized data about website usage, which helps us understand visitor preferences.

    5. Data Sharing

    We may share your information in the following cases:

    • Service Providers: With trusted third-party providers (e.g., email services, analytics tools) who assist in operating our website, bound by confidentiality agreements.
    • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
    • Social Media: If you interact with social media features, data may be shared with platforms like Twitter or LinkedIn, per their policies.

    6. Data Security

    We implement reasonable measures to protect your data, such as encryption for form submissions and secure servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You share information at your own risk.

    7. Your Rights

    You have the right to:

    • Access or request a copy of your personal data we hold.
    • Request correction of inaccurate data.
    • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
    • Opt out of newsletters or marketing communications via the unsubscribe link in emails.

    To exercise these rights, contact us at info@bloggytech.com.

    8. Third-Party Links

    Our website contains links to external sites (e.g., GitHub, social media). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before sharing information.

    9. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Updated" date. Check periodically for changes.
